Pizza Lava Toast

Source

Description

A simple toast covered with melty cheese toppings has been making the rounds on social media, but it's not just a slice of bread with some cheese melted on top. To give this toast the "lava" in its name, you actually stack two pieces of toast together to make a combination of grilled cheese and pizza toast.

Directions

  1. Toast two pieces of bread
  2. Put a cheese border around the outside of one toast (leaving the middle empty)
  3. Stack the other piece of toast on top
  4. Using the back of a spoon, push down on the center of the top toast to create a crater
  5. Fill the crater with sauce
  6. Top the whole thing with more cheese and your favorite pizza toppings (pepperoni, peppers, onions, mushrooms, etc.)
  7. Toast the whole ensemble once more to melt the cheese

Tips:

  • Perfect snack or "girl dinner"!
  • We used an oven, but a toaster oven makes it even easier and faster
