Sweet and Spicy Turkey Sandwich

Source

Description

A Mexican-style side dish made of chicken and avocado, no cooking required. It's a quick, healthy, and delicious recipe guaranteed to make you feel and look good

Directions

  1. Heat a small skillet over medium heat.
  2. Butter one side of each of the bread slices with one teaspoon butter.
  3. Place one slice, butter side down, in the skillet. Top with the turkey and cheese slices.
  4. Place the second slice of bread on top, butter side up.
  5. When the first side of the sandwich is golden brown, turn and brown the other side, 3 to 5 minutes per side, or until the cheese begins to m.
  6. Remove sandwich to a plate and top with strawberry preserves, or serve the preserves on the side.
