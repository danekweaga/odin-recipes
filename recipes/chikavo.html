Mexi-Chicken Avocado Cup

Source

Description

A Mexican-style side dish made of chicken and avocado, no cooking required. It's a quick, healthy, and delicious recipe guaranteed to make you feel and look good

Directions

  1. Stir chicken, cilantro, and chili powder together in a bowl.
  2. Arrange avocado halves on serving dish and brush with lime juice. Spoon chicken mixture into avocado halves.
